🙌 We Really Need Your Support!

As a parent of a child at Aldryngton Primary School, you are automatically a member of the PTA. You can attend meetings, vote on proposals and suggest ideas for spending or raising funds.

Even if you can't commit to a regular role, any support you can give — attending events, volunteering for an afternoon, or simply spreading the word — makes a huge difference to our school community.

About the PTA

Who we are, what we do, and how you can get involved.

What is the PTA?

Aldryngton PTA is a charity run by parent and guardian volunteers. We raise money to improve equipment and resources to enhance our children's education and the facilities in the school in general.

We promote closer links between home and school and bring staff, parents, pupils and friends together in support of our school.

💡 How Can You Get Involved?

  • Offer help before, after or at events — even occasional help is hugely valuable.
  • Attend the AGM or meetings to share ideas for fundraising events.
  • Support and attend the events we organise — your enthusiasm matters!
  • Volunteer to be a PTA Class Rep or Committee member.
  • Share your contacts or skills — come to a meeting to discuss how you could help.
  • Suggest ideas for how funds could be spent.
  • Make a donation — cash, cheque or online. Contact the Treasurer for details.

💸 Match Funding

Many employers offer match funding schemes where they will match or contribute to donations their employees make to charities. If your employer offers this scheme, please consider directing it to the Aldryngton PTA — it can double the value of your contribution at no extra cost to you!

Contact us at ptaofficers@aldryngtonpta.com for more information.

Second-Hand Uniform

Good-quality pre-loved uniform — great value for families, better for the environment.

👕 About the Uniform Sale

The PTA runs a second-hand uniform scheme selling good-quality re-usable uniform donated by parents, or sourced from unclaimed lost property.

It's a brilliant way to save money on school uniform while helping other families and reducing waste.

📦

Donating Uniform

If your child has grown out of their uniform and it's still in good condition, please consider donating it to the PTA. Drop off at the school office.

🛍️

Buying Uniform

Sales are held periodically throughout the year. Watch PTA communications for dates and availability.

📧
